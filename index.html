INCF's mission is to facilitate neuroinformatics and advance training in the field. The goal of neuroinformatics is to integrate and analyze diverse data across scales, techniques, and species to understand the brain and positively impact the health and well being of society.

Participants in the 2018 INCF Hackathon should expect to develop, implement, train others or be trained on software tools with the following themes:

  • standards and best practices
  • training and educational resources
  • open science and data sharing
  • collaborative, community-driven projects

Dates: August 7-8, 2018.

Costs: $20 CAD.

Location: Thompson House, 3650 Mc Tavish St, Montreal, QC
